Kaleidoscope

What is it?

A dynamic kaleidoscope that responds to your mouse movements, creating symmetrical patterns that evolve in real-time.

How to interact:

  • Move your mouse across the screen to control the pattern
  • The distance from the center affects the size of the shapes
  • The angle of your mouse determines the orientation
  • Each segment mirrors your movements to create perfect symmetry

What to expect:

Mesmerizing geometric patterns with neon colors that shift and transform as you explore. The kaleidoscope creates a hypnotic experience where your every movement creates a new visual composition.

Recursive Tree

What is it?

A fractal tree that grows and evolves based on your mouse position, demonstrating the beauty of recursive patterns in nature.

How to interact:

  • Move your mouse horizontally to change the branching angle
  • Move your mouse vertically to control the length of branches
  • The tree responds in real-time to your movements
  • Each branch follows mathematical patterns found in nature

What to expect:

A mesmerizing tree structure that grows from the bottom of the screen, with branches that follow the golden ratio and other natural patterns. The colors shift from root to leaves, creating a gradient effect that mimics natural growth.

Flowing Patterns

What is it?

An interactive abstract art generator where each click creates unique geometric shapes that pulse, rotate, and evolve over time, forming intricate neon patterns.

How to interact:

  • Click anywhere to spawn geometric patterns
  • Adjust pattern size to create larger or smaller shapes
  • Choose from neon, pastel, or monochrome color schemes
  • Watch as patterns rotate, pulse, and interact with each other
  • Save your favorite compositions or record animations

What to expect:

A dynamic canvas where each click spawns mesmerizing geometric shapes that transform and dance across the screen. Every pattern has unique properties - some spin slowly, others pulse with energy, creating an ever-changing abstract composition that you control.

Circle Packing

What is it?

An algorithmic art piece that creates mesmerizing patterns by packing circles of varying sizes into the available space, creating an organic, tessellated composition.

How to interact:

  • Adjust the number of circles using the slider controls
  • Control the maximum circle size to change pattern density
  • Choose between auto-color mode or specific neon colors
  • Generate new patterns with the reset button
  • Save your favorite compositions as images

What to expect:

Watch as circles of different sizes fill the space, creating intricate patterns with smooth color transitions. The result is a dynamic composition that balances randomness with mathematical precision, each generation unique and visually striking.
